Notes to Themes Makers

To take advantages of the Themes part of the app:

- you can specify theme's version/author in theme.plist by adding keys <key>Author</key> and <key>Version</key>, and content between <string></string> tags as usual.

- Thumbnails go to the theme's directory, and should be called thumb in png format.

About cdboot version

You can upgrade or downgrade cdboot file to build your ISO, by replacing cdboot into Lizard->Contents->Resources
